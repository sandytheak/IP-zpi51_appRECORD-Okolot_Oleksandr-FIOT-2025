About SimAccu

Empowering Small Businesses with Integrated Financial Intelligence.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Kyiv, SimAccu was born out of a simple observation: small business owners spend too much time switching between scattered spreadsheets and complex CRM systems.

Our mission is to provide an intuitive, secure, and accessible tool that unites financial accounting with customer relationship management.

Why SimAccu?

SimAccu isn't just another accounting app. It's a strategic partner for your business growth.

  • Unified Data: Your sales pipeline and ledgers talk to each other.
  • Security First: We use encryption to protect your sensitive financial data.
  • Mobility: Manage your business from your laptop or smartphone thanks to our fully responsive design.
